# The Bright Knight
In this game you are spawned in as a Knight. You have access to a shop, and a dungeon door. The currency is light. 
https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/3d/characters/toon-rts-units-demo-69687 is used for the knight package.
you move with wasd or the arrows, you can parry with q, you can also attack with click. You have a health bar and a mana bar
displaying how much health you have and how much mana you have. You can buy upgrades at the shop. Collect light to buy upgrades
at the shop and try to unlock it all.
